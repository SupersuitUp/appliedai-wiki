# Executive Summary

*The field in one page. The load-bearing claims a practitioner should know cold, each linking to its full treatment.*

---

This page is the compressed version of the wiki. It is the orientation a practitioner needs before they go deep: the handful of claims that, once internalized, change how you build, operate, and sell applied AI. Each bullet links to the page that argues it in full.

## The economic backdrop

The market AI is pouring into has a shape, and the shape is widening.

- The gap between people and firms that compound with AI and those that do not keeps widening. See [The Elevator Economy](/perspectives/the-elevator-economy) and, inside organizations, [The Survivor Economy](/perspectives/the-survivor-economy).
- Compute is metered, and tokens are the atomic unit of AI economics. See [The Token Economy](/perspectives/the-token-economy).
- Frontier gains are no longer monotonic, so treat models as swappable dependencies. See [The Tradeoff Era](/perspectives/the-tradeoff-era).
- Cheaper intelligence expands demand rather than shrinking it ([Jevons Paradox](/concepts/jevons-paradox)), and the clearest place to capture that today is the mid-market. See [Mid-Markets Are the AI Sweet Spot](/perspectives/mid-markets-are-the-ai-sweet-spot) and [The Services-Firm Re-Rating Threshold](/perspectives/services-firm-re-rating-threshold).

## The interface shift

The chat box was the first form factor, not the final one.

- The chat is not the product. See [The Chat Is Not the Product](/perspectives/the-chat-is-not-the-product) and [The Chatbot Trap](/perspectives/the-chatbot-trap).
- The hand-built GUI is becoming legacy as software gets generated on demand. See [The GUI Is Becoming Legacy](/perspectives/the-gui-is-becoming-legacy) and [Ephemeral Software, Precious Context](/perspectives/ephemeral-software-precious-context).
- The unit that ships work is becoming a packaged, runnable capability, not an app. See [CLIPs](/concepts/clips).

## The strategy shift

When execution gets cheap, the leverage moves up the stack.

- Strategy becomes the scarce input as execution commoditizes. See [Strategy Is the New Execution](/perspectives/strategy-is-the-new-execution).
- The durable plan is built on what will not change. See [What Will Not Change As Strategy](/perspectives/what-wont-change-as-strategy).
- Capability is uneven, so map the [Jagged Frontier](/perspectives/the-jagged-frontier) and stage adoption in [Three Waves](/perspectives/three-waves-of-ai-adoption).

## The operator shift

The constraint is no longer the model. It is the human in the loop.

- You are the bottleneck, and the bottleneck moves as you clear it. See [You Are the Bottleneck](/perspectives/you-are-the-bottleneck) and [The Moving Bottleneck](/perspectives/the-moving-bottleneck).
- Volume is free, so the discipline is curation, not production. See [The Prolific Mode](/perspectives/the-prolific-mode), [Slop Factory](/perspectives/slop-factory), and [Judgment Burnout](/perspectives/judgment-burnout).
- The failure modes are human: overtrust, overconfidence, and abdication. See [LLM Psychosis](/perspectives/llm-psychosis), [The Overconfidence Trap](/perspectives/the-overconfidence-trap), [The Judgment Line](/perspectives/the-judgment-line), and [The Permission Surface](/perspectives/the-permission-surface).
- Treat the model as a teammate you brief and supervise. See [Teammate Discipline](/disciplines/teammate-discipline).

## The craft primitives that pay off first

A few practices compound across everything above.

- [Compounding Docs](/concepts/compounding-docs): context that gets more valuable the more you write it.
- [Version-Control Your Prompts](/disciplines/version-control-your-prompts): the prompts that produced an artifact live alongside it.
- [Minimum Viable Infrastructure](/concepts/minimum-viable-infrastructure): the literacy floor required to begin.
