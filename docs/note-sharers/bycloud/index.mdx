## Worth tracking because

bycloud reads the 50-page technical reports so you do not have to, then pulls out the handful of moves that change the cost curve or the architecture. The value for an applied-AI operator is not the kernel-level detail. It is the early read on where model economics are heading: which lab cracked which bottleneck, what it does to price, and what that unlocks downstream. He is fast, technically credible, and honest about what is hype versus what is real engineering.

## Voice and stance

Technical but accessible. Comfortable in the math, but always surfaces the "why you should care" above the equations. Tracks the open-source frontier closely (DeepSeek, Kimi, GLM, Qwen) and is candid when a release is a cost-performance play rather than a benchmark win. Where the strategy consultancies on this wiki report from inside Fortune 500 engagements, bycloud reports from inside the papers.
