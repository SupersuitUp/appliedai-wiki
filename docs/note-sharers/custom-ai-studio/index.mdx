# Custom AI Studio
> Devin Karns's full-stack AI consulting firm. Architects, designs, builds, and deploys custom AI systems for mid-market clients. Two-and-a-half years in. Building toward a $100M exit, not a lifestyle business.

**Worth tracking because:** Custom AI Studio is one of the few AI consulting firms publicly thinking in terms of an enterprise-value exit rather than lifestyle business. Their operating thesis treats AI consulting as a Big-4-shaped opportunity (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) rather than a freelance one, and Devin is unusually candid about the unit economics, the engagement structure, and the strategic posture required to get there.

**Voice / stance:** Direct, builder-first, operationally specific. Quotes pricing numbers, quotes EBITDA multiples, quotes refund-rate deltas. No abstract platitudes about AI transformation.

**Where I found them:** Devin interviewed on the [Nate Herk AI Automation channel](https://www.youtube.com/@NateHerk). See [first field note](./2026-05-25-playbook-for-100m-ai-agency).

## Recurring themes
*(grows over time as patterns surface across multiple notes)*

- Mid-market ($10M-$250M revenue) as the AI era's sweet spot for consulting firms.
- The lifestyle-vs-enterprise-value decision as the most consequential structural choice an AI consulting founder makes.
- Productize the service as a signature framework; "I'll build whatever you want" reads as not-standing-on-anything.
- The engagement ladder: workshop → blueprint → project → tech partnership.
