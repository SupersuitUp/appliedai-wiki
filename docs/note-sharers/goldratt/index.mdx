# Goldratt

*The Theory of Constraints lineage (Eliyahu and Rami Goldratt). Names the binding constraint of an organization and gives the discipline of subordinating everything else to it.*

---

## Worth tracking because

Theory of Constraints is the cleanest operating logic for an AI economy, even though Goldratt predates the current wave. The core move (find the one thing that actually limits the system, then aim everything at it) is the same move applied AI lives or dies on. Goldratt's late thesis names the constraint most operators miss: management attention itself. That makes this lineage a direct ancestor of the bottleneck and leverage canon on this wiki.

## Voice and stance

Plainspoken, example-driven, allergic to local optimization. Goldratt teaches by walking one worked example all the way through (a production line, an intergenerational-poverty program, a jewelry consignment deal) until the constraint is undeniable. The recurring instruction is to stop doing more and start subordinating.
