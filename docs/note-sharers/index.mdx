# Note Sharers

*Firms whose applied-AI fieldwork we track and process into structured notes. Sourced from public publications. We attribute everything; we endorse nothing we have not field-tested ourselves.*

---

## What lives here

Each note-sharer firm gets its own subdirectory. Inside, a firm profile (`index.mdx`) holds the stance and recurring themes. Per-source notes are dated and topical: each one captures the specific frameworks, claims, or quotes we lifted from one piece of their published material.

The point is not to mirror every firm we read. The point is to track firms whose thinking holds up over time, attribute their claims correctly, and surface where their fieldwork reinforces or extends concepts already on this wiki.

## How to read a field note

- The frontmatter names the source: firm, date, URL, format.
- The "What we lifted" section is the list of insights that survived triage. Each one names where it lands in this wiki (new concept, reinforces an existing page, or stays as a bullet here).
- Quotes are verbatim. Commentary is editorial and clearly separated from the firm's voice.
- Nothing in a field note implies we have validated the claim in our own engagements unless we say so explicitly.
