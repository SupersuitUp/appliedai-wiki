*A bucket for applied-AI fieldwork heard in private settings where the source consents to the insights being shared but not their name. Frameworks travel, attribution stays private.*

---

## Worth tracking because

Some of the sharpest applied-AI playbook detail does not get published. It surfaces in private rooms: late-night strategy sessions, advisory dinners, peer-to-peer founder conversations between consultancy operators. The frameworks are real, the numbers are real, the buyer behavior is real, and the field deserves the lift even when the person who shared cannot be named.

This bucket exists for those moments. Each note inside is anonymized to firm and founder, but the framework, pricing anchors, and tactical moves are reported faithfully.

## Voice and stance

Practitioner voice, not pundit voice. Detail-heavy on deal mechanics (retainer sizes, scope shape, who approves the budget) and light on theory. Each note triangulates one consultancy operator's lived experience inside the buyer relationships they currently run.
