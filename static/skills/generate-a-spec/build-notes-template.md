# Generate-a-Spec Build Notes

Working notes for one run. Capture raw answers here during the interview, then write the spec from them. Keep the messy detail; the spec is the clean projection.

## Q2. The outcome it serves
(why now, cost of not having it, money made or saved)

## Q3. Current reality
(step by step, every tool and workaround, where it hurts)

## Q4. The dream
(the day once it works, the one thing left to do)

## Q5. Core features and behaviors
(one capability per bullet: what it does / success looks like / what happens when it goes wrong)

## Q6. Users and data
(who uses it, data in and out, where it lives, what it connects to)

## Q7. Constraints and non-negotiables
(budget, timeline, privacy, tone, must-nevers)

## Q8. Open questions
(what they are unsure about, where they defer to an expert)

## Q9. Success and scale
(how they know it works, volume now and later)
